Mission


हमारा ध्येय है ; भारतीय शास्त्रीय संगीत को जन मानस तक पहुंचाना।Our mission is to promote Indian Classical Music to every human being.

Vision

Create and provide a common platform to art aspirants & artists to come together and share their strength among them and spread uniqueness of every art. Bring together the humanity through art.

We follow the Ethics of 4 Thumb Rules

1. Listen

Listen / श्रवण
The first step to climb ladder if music is to Listen and absorb

2. Learn

Learn /अध्ययन
Once the musical notes started percolating its time to learn. You need a teacher /the Guru, to
carve musical notes in you mind.

3. Play

Play/ अभ्यास 
Musical carving is now getting ready for exhibition. Just play around it for yourself and let others
realize the musical calm and peace through.

4. Master

Master / प्रवीण
Get confidence, perform in the public and spread music as mu h as you can.
Share your skills with others and be master.
